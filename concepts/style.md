# Aesthetic and Style

The game's visual style is inspired by early 3D consoles like the original PlayStation, with low-poly models, pixelated textures and visible seams. Rooms use placeholder textures, developer tool overlays, and glitch effects to evoke the feeling of unfinished software.

The overall mood should be eerie yet nostalgic, mixing the charm of retro games with the uncanny feeling of something that was never meant to be seen. Ambient sounds and distorted music underscore the decaying digital world.
