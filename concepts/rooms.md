# Rooms in the Glitch Museum

## Scrapped Games
- Play prototypes of cancelled games.
- Explore levels that end abruptly.
- NPCs mention the developers that vanished mid-development.

## Corrupted Files
- Files that mutate while you interact with them.
- Images rewrite themselves.
- Music becomes progressively distorted.

## AI Experiments
- NPCs generated by different AI models.
- Some talk normally while others speak in glitchy loops.

## Internet Archaeology
- Old forum posts, half-finished wikis, and ARG fragments.
- Deleted videos and other digital fossils.
