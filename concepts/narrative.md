# Narrative of the Glitch Museum

The museum starts as a curated space of digital artifacts, but the visitor soon realizes that something is off: there are no other visitors and the exhibits react to their presence. Messages warn that the museum has been abandoned, and some NPCs whisper about a broken game that trapped them.

As you progress deeper, the museum itself starts to break down — walls flicker, assets disappear, and internal developer notes appear. NPCs begin to malfunction and the environment becomes increasingly unstable.

Eventually, you discover that the museum is inside the unfinished game you are playing. You are an unwanted presence in a development build, and the only way out is to find the hidden exit through the most corrupted exhibit.
