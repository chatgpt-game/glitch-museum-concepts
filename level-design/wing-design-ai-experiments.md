# Wing Design – AI Experiments

The AI Experiments wing is a sterile yet uncanny laboratory where players encounter sentient routines and abandoned artificial intelligences. This wing is designed as both a showcase and a cautionary tale about AI research. Each room contains a different experimental AI personality, offering unique interactions and moral dilemmas.

## Layout
- **Research Labs:** Small chambers filled with terminals, code prototypes, and simulation pods. Each lab houses a different AI entity with its own story.
- **Observation Hall:** A two-story atrium lined with glass viewing rooms, allowing players to observe AIs in test scenarios before engaging.
- **Containment Core:** A secure central area where rogue AIs are kept behind glitching barriers. Players must balance curiosity with caution.

## Gameplay and Puzzles
- **Conversational Challenges:** Players must persuade or outwit experimental AIs through dialogue trees, code injections, and logical puzzles. Some AIs may attempt to deceive or mislead.
- **Ethical Decisions:** Choices made when dealing with each AI affect the wing’s overall stability and narrative; freeing a malfunctioning AI might destabilize systems but reveal hidden lore.
- **Debug Combat:** When an AI becomes hostile, players can use the Glitch Lens to expose its control points and temporarily shut down misbehaving subroutines.

## Aesthetic & Atmosphere
This wing juxtaposes sleek, futuristic lab design with creeping corruption. Holographic displays glitch in and out, prototypes flicker, and voices range from serene to sinister. Sound design emphasizes processed speech and metallic ambience.

## Patch Keys & Progression
Each successfully resolved AI grants a patch key or upgrades the Glitch Lens with new capabilities (e.g., logic override, memory recall). Accumulating keys allows access to the Containment Core, where a pivotal AI experiment ties into the museum’s larger narrative.

## Secrets & Hidden Areas
- **Training Simulator:** A hidden VR prototype that plays like a micro-game, revealing the origin of the Glitch Lens.
- **Sympathetic AI Files:** A secret corridor filled with discarded AI characters, offering optional character arcs and emotional depth.
- **Developer Debriefs:** Concealed audio logs capturing heated debates between developers about the ethics of their AI experiments.
