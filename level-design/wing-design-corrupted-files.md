# Wing Design – Corrupted Files

This wing plunges players into the heart of data rot and glitchy chaos. Corrupted Files is a warped, unpredictable environment where every asset is decayed. Bits of geometry drift, textures smear across surfaces, and audio stutters; the entire wing is intentionally unstable. Players must use the Glitch Lens and patch keys to navigate shifting pathways and rescue fragments of lost information.

## Layout
- **Shifting Corridors:** Hallways randomly reconfigure, forcing players to rely on visual landmarks and glitch markers to navigate.
- **Corruption Zones:** Rooms filled with hazardous glitch goo or fractured platforms. Entering these areas increases the instability meter until players stabilize them.
- **Memory Dumps:** Library-like spaces containing clusters of corrupted documents and audio logs. Solving mini-puzzles here cleanses the corruption and reveals lore.

## Mechanics
- **Instability Meter:** A gauge that rises when the player is exposed to corruption. If it maxes out, the wing forcibly resets to its most unstable state, scrambling the layout.
- **Purification Puzzles:** Using the Glitch Lens to trace data flows and reconnect broken circuits. Completing these puzzles repairs sections of the wing, granting access to deeper areas.
- **Patch Key Nodes:** Specific terminals require patch keys to purge high-level corruption. Using a key triggers a dramatic visual overhaul as geometry and textures snap back into place.

## Aesthetic & Atmosphere
Expect a surreal mix of digital decay: textures shift and blur, colors bleed, and vertex distortion is rampant. Static and distorted audio create unease. Lighting flickers between neon glows and deep shadows, emphasizing the unpredictable nature of corruption.

## Progression & Rewards
As players stabilize areas, the instability meter resets and permanently unlocks shortcuts. Purified memory dumps contain valuable lore entries and hints about the museum’s origin. Fully cleansing the wing may reveal a secret about the source of the corruption itself.

## Secrets & Hidden Challenges
- **Glitch Geysers:** Optional arenas where players deliberately overload the instability meter to fight waves of corrupted entities, earning rare patch keys.
- **Data Ghost Trails:** Hidden pathways that become visible only when players record and replay their own movements using the Glitch Lens.
- **Developer Backdoor:** A concealed debug portal that teleports players to a behind-the-scenes area showing the corruption simulation in wireframe.
