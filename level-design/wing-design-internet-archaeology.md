# Wing Design – Internet Archaeology

This wing is a nostalgic, labyrinthine archive of the early Internet. Players explore exhibits styled after 1990s‑2000s web pages, forums, and chat rooms, all preserved within the museum. The wing celebrates digital history while hiding puzzles in outdated code and forgotten communities.

## Layout
- **GeoCities Gallery:** A maze of virtual “web neighborhoods” with vibrant colors, animated GIFs, and MIDI music. Each page is a room with broken links and hidden secrets.
- **Forum Archives:** Multi-room exhibits that emulate old message boards. Players must piece together conversations to solve puzzles and uncover lost URLs.
- **Chatroom Plaza:** A central hub with floating chat bubbles and NPC avatars representing archived users. These avatars respond with quotes drawn from actual posts.

## Mechanics
- **Hyperlink Navigation:** Instead of doors, players click on glowing hyperlinks to move between rooms. Some links are dead ends until revived with patch keys.
- **Source Code Puzzles:** Viewing the page’s HTML and CSS through the Glitch Lens reveals hidden messages and commands. Players can edit tags to modify the environment (e.g., changing a `<div>` color to reveal an invisible platform).
- **Bandwidth Constraints:** The wing simulates dial-up speeds; interactions are delayed unless players find modems to boost connection speed.

## Aesthetic & Atmosphere
Expect a cacophony of bright backgrounds, tiled textures, pixel fonts, and looping GIFs. Audio is composed of low-fi samples, boot-up sounds, and dial-up noise. Lighting is neon and chaotic, evoking the early web’s DIY aesthetic.

## Patch Keys & Progression
Patch keys here represent “deprecated technologies” (e.g., Flash plugins, Java applets). Collecting these keys restores broken hyperlinks and unlocks deeper layers, such as hidden personal blogs and early social networks.

## Secrets & Hidden Areas
- **Easter Egg Hunt:** Hidden pixel art icons that reference classic memes and early Internet culture. Collecting them unlocks a secret “meme vault.”
- **Archive Dive:** A secret basement filled with ancient servers. Players can physically navigate server racks to recover lost websites.
- **ARG Hooks:** QR codes and ciphers hidden in markup that direct players to real-world web pages, expanding the game’s ARG component.
